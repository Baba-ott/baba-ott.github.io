ICT field of study

Information and communication technology (ICT) is a term enclosing all the technologies and services included in computing, information management, telecommunications provision, and the internet. Those technologies all deal with the transmission and reception of facts of some kind. ICT permeates all components of life, providing newer, better, and faster ways for human beings to interact, network, seek help, gain access to records and learn. The information and communications technology (ICT) sector is one of the most exciting and thrilling areas to work and not only is the pay structure good but the sector involves an extensive number of areas like programming, business consulting, and development, and sales and marketing roles. Initially, graduates can only find jobs in the core domains like IT & Telecom. But now they are also making their career in the Financial Services, Real Estate, Manufacturing and other industries.
